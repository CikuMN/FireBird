About

The story behind the stories

Some practitioners are good at one thing. A rare few are formed by many things — and carry all of them into every room they enter. I am a film and television producer, an award-winning lecturer, a curriculum architect, a book editor, a public speaker, a communications strategist, a pastor, and a tireless advocate for the next generation of women in sport.

"The right story, told the right way, can change the course of a life, a campaign, or a nation."

My work has taken me from the broadcast trucks of FIFA World Cup Qualifiers to the storytelling workshops of Kakuma Refugee Camp. From the stage at TED Talks Nairobi to the lecture halls of the Africa Digital Media Institute. From shaping national TVET curriculum standards to editing the manuscripts of seven published books.

I am based in Nairobi and available globally — for long-term partnerships, short-term consultancies, speaking engagements, and everything in between. I bring to every engagement not just expertise, but a genuine conviction that what we make and how we communicate it matters deeply.

Proverbs 22:29 — "Do you see someone skilled in their work? They will serve before kings."
